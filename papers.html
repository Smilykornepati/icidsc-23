Paper Submission Guidelines


  • All submitted papers will be peer reviewed.
  • The submitted papers should not have been published or under review previously in any journal or conference.
  • Preferred length of the paper is between 3-5 pages. It must include an Abstract (no more than 150 words) and 3-5 Keywords.
  • The submitted papers should strictly adhere to the template given in the conference webpage.
  • For every paper a maximum of 4 authors are allowed.
  • The plagiarism should be less than 15% for every paper submitted to the conference.
  • Full paper must be written in British English, typed using Times New Roman (normal style and font size 12) and in MS-Word. Page size should be A4, single column with 2.5 cm margin on both sides with single line spacing. All pages of the manuscript (including Tables and Figures) should be numbered.
  • Papers submitted and presented at the conference will be published in the conference proceedings, subject to their acceptance by the review committee, immediately after the conference, which will be available to view and download from the conference webpage.
  • Each conference proceedings will have a unique digit International Standard International Standard Book Number (ISBN) code.
